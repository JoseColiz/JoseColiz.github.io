Fifa 19

Currently, this is my favorite game that is available. When I have free time away from school work or there are no sports games on tv,
then I will spend my time playing the game. The Fifa series has been one of my favorite franchises in for a while now. There are
several game modes that I enjoy playing. Pro Clubs is one where you make your own player and get to make him better after playing in
games. You make a team with friends and try to go through the ranks together and get as high as you can. Another mode is Ultimate Team.
in this mode you collect player cards and form your "dream team" to compete with players from all over the world.

Apex: Legends

This is the other game that I play when I get tired of Fifa. Apex is a free-to-play FPS (first person shooter) battle royale game. 60 players
are in a game together in teams of three. The teams roam around the map collecing their wepons and material and try to eliminate the
other teams and become the champions. The game was one of the most popular games upon its release and had 2.5 million players within
the first day, 25 million players in the first week, and reached 50 million one month after release. Here is the gameplay trailer
