Manchester United

Manchester United is my favorite soccer/football (whatever you want to call it) team. I have supported this team for years now.
Their history, the legends that have played for the club, the colors, Old Trafford, all led me to this team. My favorite player of all time,
Cristiano Ronaldo, started to grow into the legend he is in Manchester. The treble winning 1998-99 team, although I did not get to
watch them play, is one of my favorite teams ever, along with the Champions League winning team in 2007-08.

Chicago Bears

My favorite football team is the Chicago Bears. I have been watching this team and following them ever since I was a kid.
Currently the team is on the rise after many years of being very bad. Last season the team was finally able to make it to
the playoffs for the first time in years. With players like Mitch Trubisky, Tarik Cohen, Khalil Mack, and Eddie Jackson, myself
and all of the other fans have something exciting to root for and watch on sundays.
