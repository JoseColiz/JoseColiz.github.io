Why I love the sport

I grew up watching and playing the sport so it has always been in my life. As I grew older I started not only watching it casually, but also analyzing games and wanting to learn more about tactics and how the game is played. The variety of formations that teams play and the thinking that goes in before each match is exciting to me. Of course another thing has to be the skills and control that the best players have.

Favorite Team!

My favorite team is Manchester United, the Red Devils. I began supporting Man U years ago. Their history, the legends that have played for the club, the colors, Old Trafford, all led me to this team. My favorite player of all time, Cristiano Ronaldo, started to grow into the legend he is in Manchester. The treble winning 1998-99 team, although I did not get to watch them play, is one of my favorite teams ever along with the Champions League winning team in 2007-08.
