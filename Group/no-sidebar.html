About Us

Founded in August of 2015, Search.Find.Love strives to find forever homes for North Central Illinois' furry friends who are currently sitting in shelters and who are in desperate need loving homes. Our team consists of three Operations Management and Information Systems majors who are using their knowledge in both information technology, and operations management to efficiently solve these problems. We collaborate with most animal shelters across North Central Illinois but if you have a shelter in mind we will gladly reach out to help find the cute pup that will warm your heart and home. Please submit a form on our adopt page to gather more information or call us at 815-753-1285.
