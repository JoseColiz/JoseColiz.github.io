Top 3 CDs

News of the World
News of the World was the sixth studio album for Queen. The album included some of the most iconic songs in our lifetime and for queen in 'We Are the Champions' and 'We Will Rock You'
A Night at the Opera
Bohemian Rhapsody is the star song off the album and the main album portrayed during the movie 'Bohemian Rhapsody'. It was also just recently added into Grammys Hall of Fame.
Queen II
The second album, and an underappreciated album that many beleive that this was the album that began to show the signature sound that Queen would eventually be known for.
